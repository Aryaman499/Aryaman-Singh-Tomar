ATK Constructions

ATK Constructions is a construction company headquartered in Punjab, India whose businesses span the sectors of Engineering & Construction, Real Estate, Infrastructure, Urban development & Management. The ATK group of companies comprises ATK Ltd. and its subsidiaries ATK Real Estate Ltd., ATK Infrastructure Co. Ltd., Lavasa Corporation Ltd., Steiner AG in Switzerland and Highbar Technologies Ltd.

Founded by Industrialists Aman,Tarun and Kaju in 2015, ATK Ltd. executes large-scale civil engineering and infrastructure projects such as hydel power plants, nuclear power plants, expressways and highways, tunnels and bridges. ATK Constructions currently serves the infrastructure sectors of transportation, power and water. ATK Constructions was the first construction company in India to be certified ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and OHSAS 18001 for its quality, environmental and occupational health and safety management systems
